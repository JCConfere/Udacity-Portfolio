About Me

Born and raised in West Virginia, I have a unique mix of experience that I feel helps me to bring a fresh perspective as a web developer.

Fifteen years of retail experience taught me marketing, presentation, customer service, analytics, and communication skills.

Ten years of work in the construction industry taught me how visualize a project from its conception, the steps necessary to bring it to fruition, the importance of the "heavy lifting", and how industry standards and best practices lay the foundations for success.

As a web developer, my goals are to provide meticulous attention to detail, a dedication that allows my clients to feel at ease about their web presence, and a proactive vision that allows new resources to be utilized in an efficienct manner that is also pragmatic in the application of those advancements
